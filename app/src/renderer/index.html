📋 What to do next — follow these steps in UniFi Connect
1Click Upload to UniFi Connect below — your browser will open to your console. Log in if prompted.
2In the device list, click on a Display to open its detail panel on the right.
3Click the ⚙ gear icon in the right panel. Verify Display Mode is set to Android App, then click Manage Apps.
4In the Manage Apps dialog, click the upload icon (box with arrow, top right). Navigate to your download folder and select the APK file.
5After upload, close Manage Apps. In the App dropdown, select the newly uploaded version. The display will reboot and install. ✅
